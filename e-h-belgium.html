Tomorrowland Music Festival
Date of organization

Tomorrowland is an electronic music festival held for the first time in the town of Boom, Belgium. Tomorrowland was first held in 2005, and has since become one of the largest and most popular music festivals in the world.



The festival usually takes place in the last week of July.Prepare your self for the most excited festival of the year in Belgium.

Festival Details

Known for being a show full of energy and themes in phantasmagorical style (like the movie Avatar). In the first years (2004) since the festival started it has grown into one of the largest electronic music festivals in the world and leaves it without competition in the field.


Every year, this festival is organized and a lot of manpower and effort is invested in the decoration, which brings a lot of experience to the attendees at this festival.



Besides, Tomorrowland is also surrounded by many beautiful lakes, hills and fields of Belgium.



What is Tomorrowland Stage? There are about 20 large and small stages according to the style and personality of each performer. Some may perform every day, some perform on a different day-to-day basis. Therefore, attendees could not see all the performances on the line-up. Each stage is carefully decorated, and each stage has its own musical style.




You should wear clothes with bright colors, suitable for the festive atmosphere here, and not too tight to be able to move and dance here easily. It is strongly recommended that you do not wear denim, because it is not good when you get wet by rain or sweat. Clothing with political and religious colors is also prohibited. Shoes and sandals should be easy to move because it can take you up to 15 - 30 minutes to travel from the campsite to the festival site.

The weather here can be very sunny, if you are camping and are outside often, it is advisable to wear sunglasses. Sunscreen is also essential. Bring water and drink water often as you can lose a lot of water, making you feel tired.
