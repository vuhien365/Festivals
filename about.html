Ati Atihan

Ati-Atihan festival is held by the Kalibo people of Panay island, Philippines in the 3rd week of January, to honor the child Lord Santo Nino - the guardian deity for the whole country.
Sumidagawa Hanabi Taikai

The festival is held on the banks of the Sumida River in Tokyo on the Sunday of July every year.
Harbin International Ice and Snow festival

The Harbin Ice and Snow Festival takes place from January 5 to the end of February.
Mid-Autumn Festival
