Chelsea Flower Show
Date of organization

The Chelsea Flower Show runs for 5 days in May. Because of the popularity of the exhibition, people often have to buy tickets almost a year in advance.

Festival Details

Chelsea is an affluent borough in South West London, adjacent to the River Thames. Every year in May, people in this district eagerly welcome the Chelsea flower show - Chelsea Flower Show.





This is the most prestigious exhibition of RHS, and probably also the most famous exhibition of flowers and horticulture in the world. The forerunner of the Chelsea Flower Show was The Great Spring Show, first held in 1862 at the RHS's flower garden in Kensington.


In 1912 the exhibition was moved to the Royal Hospital Chelsea because the venue was more spacious. Royal Hospital Chelsea itself is a very beautiful estate located right in the city of London. This is a military property, currently used as a nursing home for about 300 retired officers.



The first two days of the exhibition are dedicated to RHS members and experts and artisans in floriculture, floriculture, and horticulture. The next three days are open to the public. Chelsea Flower Show not only has flowers in Europe but also flowers from all over the world.






In the 1930s, importing these things from halfway around the world was quite a feat. Today's flower exhibition is not to mention because of many advanced techniques, so every year there are countless new flowers that are not on the market yet.


Because of the great prestige of this exhibition, the award-winning artists at the Chelsea Flower Show will often have an open career path, make a lot of money, and have many contracts. So this is an opportunity for many artists and flower brands to show off.


Chelsea Flower Show in general is not easy to visit, but if you have the opportunity, it should not be missed at all. This is also a flower exhibition that always has the presence of the Queen and the Royal Family, as well as many famous personalities. In addition, the organizers also know how to make money with the accompanying catering and resort services. It's kind of expensive, but it's going to be an aristocratic, one-of-a-kind experience.
