Morning Calm
Date of organization

The best time to explore the Morning Calm flower garden is winter.
The garden is open to visitors on weekdays but will close at 9pm on weekdays and 11pm on Saturdays

Festival detail

The Korean Morning Calm flower garden was first designed in 1996. Professor Han San-kyung of Sahmyook University was the one who came up with the idea with the desire to spread the beauty of the soul of the Korean people through plants. flowers. Not only planting a flower garden, the professor also wanted to create a special art space.

Korean Morning Calm Flower Garden is one of the beautiful scenic coordinates that tourists visit when they have the opportunity to visit the land of kimchi. Everyone knows that Korea is famous for its flowers, flower fields and flower streets. But when you come to Morning Calm, you will once again be satisfied with the beautiful scenery.


Morning Calm is a private flower garden located in Gapyeong district, Gyeonggi province. For locals and tourists alike, this is one of the most beautiful flower gardens in Korea. Unlike many other gardens, Morning Calm is a combination of flowers and herbs and classical and modern art space, creating a special and unique impression.


Morning Calm Korea Flower Garden has a large area with more than 5000 species of plants planted throughout the campus. In particular, there are about 300 species of flowers and plants indigenous to the land of kimchi, considered the "soul" of the beautiful Baekdusan mountains.


Among the small gardens of Morning Calm, the most prominent is the Sukgeun garden. This garden is designed to look like the Korean peninsula. Here, the flower beds are carefully cared for and selected, very beautiful, symbolizing the harmony and reunion of South and North Korea.


Morning Calm owns an extremely large area, divided into 20 small gardens inside. Each garden will have its own theme, such as Hometown House Garden, Bonsai Garden, Moonlight Garden, Sky Path, Garden of Eden, etc. Depending on the theme, each garden chooses suitable plants and flowers, ensuring no Time is always in love, also poetry throughout the four seasons.






Beautiful, poetic and brilliant all year round, Morning Calm flower garden is always a beautiful destination in Korea that is loved by many tourists. Not only local tourists, international tourists but also photographers often come here to save beautiful moments of nature and flowers.
