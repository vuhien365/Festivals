The Bruges Ice Sculpture Festival
Date of organization

The Bruges Ice Sculpture Festival is held annually from November to January next year. The time period is quite long because this is one of the four most famous festivals in Belgium.



The festival is held in Bruges - the largest and most ancient city in Belgium.

Festival Details

When winter comes, Belgium becomes even more vibrant and bustling when this is the time when the ice sculpture festival in Bruges takes place. Because of the attraction as well as the uniqueness of the ice sculpture festival in Bruges, every year, millions of tourists flock to Belgium to participate in the festival.


Did you know, to serve each ice sculpture festival season in Bruges, the festival organizers had to prepare 300 tons of ice and 400 tons of pure snow. Only with such a volume of ice and snow is it enough to serve a large number of visitors to participate in the festival. Moreover, the organizers also prepare the right exhibition space and temperature so that each work does not melt. Surely the thoughtfulness in the preparation for the ice sculpture festival in Bruges will make visitors satisfied.



Every season, the ice sculpture festival in Bruges usually features about 100 beautiful works from artists. From the hard raw tapes, with their skillful hands, the artisans have "transformed" them into masterpieces. Not only that, visitors will also experience sculpting on ice through fun activities at the festival.



To create interesting, fresh feeling for visitors, each ice sculpture festival season in Bruges will be a separate sculpture theme.






The festival is held in the heart of Station Square, close to the bustling city center of Bruges. Coming here, visitors will have the opportunity to admire the beautiful works meticulously carved to the smallest detail. Created by a combination of light and sculpture, the works at this festival are sure to make all emotions burst with its sublime beauty.
